Help The Sparks Community Prosper.


We are an independant non-profit organisation which strives to provide quality first hand work experience for programmers from all feilds. We try our best to help thousands of college students every year to experience actual work standard projects, in an actual work standard deadline.
We hope to pave a better way and make possible a better future for our upcoming youth. But we cannot do this without your help. Running a non-profit org is morally rich, but economically taxing to the lives of those who try to continue doing this work.
Please consider helping us out. We appreciate every token of gratitude you send our way, no matter how big or small, from the bottom of our hearts.

It's a warm feeling in our hearts when we see people genuinely greatful in our community, and especially those who in return help us with what they can share. It's a win-win. The world grows better with every intern we train, and every paise you send us :)

Let's all keep growing!
Sincerely, The Sparks Foundation.
